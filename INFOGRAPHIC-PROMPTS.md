## USAGE NOTES FOR NANO BANANA PRO

- **Aspect ratio**: All prompts designed for 16:9

- **Typography**: Bold, readable headers. Mix of medieval-inspired and modern clean fonts works well
- **Icons**: Simple, recognizable. Flat design or line art style
- **Tone**: Fun, slightly irreverent, but informative. The game is about laughing at peasant deaths while having strategic fun

Feel free to adjust prompts based on Nano Banana Pro's specific capabilities and your preferred visual style!
